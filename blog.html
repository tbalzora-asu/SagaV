Mix It Like a Major Arcana

Five flavor archetypes—sweet, salty, sour, bitter, and umami—reimagined as bold, alcohol-free rituals.

The Sun (Sweet)

Flavor: Golden peach, honey, and vanilla blossom with sparkling water.

Profile: Bright, uplifting, approachable.

Serving idea

A radiant figure holding a chalice, representing joy and optimism.

  • Glass: chilled rocks or coupe
  • Ice: large cube (or none for sparkling)
  • Garnish: seasonal herb or citrus peel
